                                                     Pang Game
 In this game player fires the balloon, large balloon destroyed and divided into some small balloons and so on.

 Steps:

 1) player and its movement using transform.translate
 2) Fire Spawning when spacebar pressed
 3) balloon movement using rigid body functions
 4) Spawning balloon when hit with fire using trigger

 I face difficulty in spawning balloon when hit with fire
 I enjoyed it because it takes deep thinking and lots of learning.
